About

Started in 2020, MacTricks is bringing you the hottest Apple insider tips and tricks to get the most of your devices.

This project started in a garage, much like Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak did when designing the first Apple computer. Our ambition to provide our users with the best Apple news and content is truly driven by our interest in the products, rather than money or publicity.

We will continue to provide the best information for all Apple enthusiasts for as long as we're around. We plan to add more employees to our team very shortly, so stay tuned for more content every day!

Feel free to reach out below to us if you're interested in joining our team, or if you have any valuable feedback to provide us!
